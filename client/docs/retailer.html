4. Retailer

Carly, Joe, and Alex each work for Walmart, Lotte Mart, and Tesco in China, respectively. Each retailer receives the shipments of honey from China House, Inc. within a few days. They all follow the same procedure of taking posession of the popcode with their Tracer apps.

Retailers are the final actor in the chain who are officially recorded on Thingchain. After the three workers complete the transaction of custody for the shipment, it is up to the individual retail store to set their pricing, run inventory checks, etc. They will not be further involved in Skuchain's products other than acting as the seller in the Smartbonds between the distributor and the last recorded custodian of the goods. Now, all that is left is for the consumer to purchase the goods.
